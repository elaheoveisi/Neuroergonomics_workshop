About This Research Area

Submissions focus on research related to Beyond the Survey: Real-Time Physiological Sensing for Human–Machine Teaming, and intelligent decision-support systems. These topics are important for understanding how humans interact with advanced technologies in complex and safety-critical environments.

Workshop timeline: July 13 (Boston University).
Organizer: Elahe Oveisi (elahe.oveisi.okstate.edu), Dr Hemmanth Manjunatha (Hemanth.manjunatha@okstate.edu)

Research Fields

EEG and Brain Signals

EEG data can be used to study cognitive workload, attention, errors, and human performance during complex tasks.

Robots and Intelligent Systems

Robots and AI systems can assist humans in navigation, decision-making, rescue operations, and safety-critical missions.
